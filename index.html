Places Must Visit

select city walk

I had vsited in 4 May, 2022

Select CITYWALK is a shopping centre located in the Saket District Centre (Sector-6, Pushp Vihar), in Saket, New Delhi. The mall was developed by Select Infrastructure, a joint venture between the Select group and the Aarone Group. It was opened to the public in October 2007. It has am awesome indoor area which is decorated beautifully with fairy lights on various festitvities. Decorations of the respective festival are also added like on christmas many raindeers, sleighs and gift boxs are put as hangings on the roof, usually there is a huge christmas tree in the center of the indoor and outdoor area also. There is a outdoor area in this mall which has beautiful statues, benches in the shape of different animals and flower beds which are great for clicking pictures. Walking outdoors in this mall feels very nice especially during festivities.

Taj Mahal

i had visited in 2015

The Taj Mahal is an Islamic ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal; it also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself. It was built with white marble and had precious stones embedded on the walls but the britishers took them. It has something written in a different language at the edge of every window. It is one of the 7 wonders of the wonders of the world.
